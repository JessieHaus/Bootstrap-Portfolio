About Me

I'm a full-stack web developer who enjoys developing React applications. I graduated from the University of Utah's Coding Bootcamp in July of 2018, and have been working as a web developer ever since. Before that, I traveled the country with AmeriCorps performing service work for various nonprofits. Some of the projects I worked on in AmeriCorps included rebuilding houses destroyed by tornados in Oklahoma with Habitat for Humanity, building hiking trails in the Rockies, building ropes courses in Arkansas for 4H, and filing taxes for low-income individuals and families with VITA in Kansas City.
